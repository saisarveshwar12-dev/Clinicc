Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a dental checkup?

We recommend a professional checkup and cleaning every 6 months to maintain optimal oral health.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by our licensed specialists.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, we reserve spots daily for dental emergencies. Call us immediately if you're in pain.

Does root canal treatment hurt?

With modern anesthesia, a root canal is absolutely comfortable and feels no different than getting a regular filling.
